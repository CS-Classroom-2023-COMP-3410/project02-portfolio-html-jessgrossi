Introduction

Hi, I'm Jessica Grossi, a creative enthusiast and productivity lover. I've been bullet journaling for 4 years and find it to be the perfect way to combine art with organization. Additionally, for me journalling is a way to calm down and relax.

I stumbled upon bullet journaling early during COVID-19 pandemic, 2020, as a way to ingore the state of the world and entertain myself as I was no longer able to see friends. What started as a productivity tool quickly became a creative outlet and a source of joy.

This website is my way of inspiring others to organize their lives while embracing their creativity. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned journaler, there's something here for everyone.

When I'm not journaling, you'll find me experimenting with digital art, reading fantasy novels, or exploring local coffee shops.

I'd love to hear from you! Feel free to share your thoughts, questions, or journal designs with me at jmgrossi11@gmail.com or through the contact page linked above.
